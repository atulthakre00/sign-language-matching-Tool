What is Sign Language?

A language that employs signs made with the hands and other movements, including facial expressions and postures of the body, used primarily by people who are deaf. There are many different sign languages as, for example, British and American sign languages. British sign language (BSL) is not easily intelligible to users of American sign language (ASL).

Sign Language Symbols

Parameters that make Sign Language Symbols different:

  • Handshape
  • Palm Orientation
  • Location
  • Movement
  • Facial Expressions
